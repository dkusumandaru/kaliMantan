 ABOUT 

kaliMantan is adventure game like Ular Tangga . This game tell us about crossing xmantan Island with mathematics syimbol . To move from point into the other poitn doesn't use dice but using how many Math Quest that can resolve on Turn Time that you setting.
How the first player that can go to finish is a Winner Player.

 HOW TO PLAY 

  • 1st

    Go to PLAYGAME page, and click (playbutton).

    2nd

    Input Username player 1 and player2, then click GO to begin the game.

    3rd

    Click Player 1 Turn to show Math Quest.

    4th

    Click check to check your answer, if your answer is true you got 1 point to moving your position, and if your answer is not true you got -1 point to move your position.

    5th

    Click next to show more quest, it's can do until your turn time is over.

    6th

    after turn Player 1 is over, next will be continue by Player 2.

    7th

    Turn player will be looping until the MainTime that you setting is over, and show how is that can be move most far.
